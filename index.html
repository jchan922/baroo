BAROO -- the bowl that Buddhist monks use for their meals.

Our food is served with love and respect to nature and people. We use local, sustainable, and organic ingredients, to capture the spirit of mother nature and push the boundaries of what's possible.

We are an experimental concept kitchen that explores fermentation. Our menu often changes based on the season, local sustainability, and our imaginations.
